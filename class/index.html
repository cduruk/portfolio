Health Kiosk

For my Human-Computer Interaction Capstone project, we designed and implemented the front-end for a Health Kiosk that was designed to be used by seniors living in community centers.

The project included very unique challenges as our target demographic was very technology averse. Over many iterations and constant testing, we finalized a design that was well received by the seniors.

Lectern ReDesign

For my Human Factors class, I came up with a redesign of the lectern systems at Carnegie Mellon. The lecterns enable users to present materials from their laptops or other devices but are generally considered hard to use.

My redesign helps users to accomplish their task rather than just configuring the system to fit their needs. The concept got high praises from the instructor.

Mobile Course Management

This project was a group project in which we were asked to design an actual product after conducting extensive user research and using various design tools such as interviews, personas and interviews.

In the end, we came up with a iPhone Web-Application that is loosely based on the iUI framework. The web application aims to be a iPhone version of the extensively used Blackboard Course Management System used at Carnegie Mellon University.
